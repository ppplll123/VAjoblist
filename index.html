📖 How to Use This Dashboard

Your Workflow:

  1. Start with New (24h) tab — these are freshly posted jobs from the last 24 hours with high scores
  2. Then go to Top Jobs — all high-scoring jobs sorted by relevance
  3. Check Professor tab — academic positions at UAE universities
  4. Finally Browse for lower-scored jobs that might still be interesting

How to Apply:

  • Click the job title to open it on LinkedIn
  • After applying, click the ☐ checkbox to mark it as done
  • Your target: 40 external applications per day (see Cherry tab for your progress)

Feedback Buttons (very important!):

  • Wrong Role — if the job title doesn't match Peter's profile (e.g., HR, nursing, construction)
  • Wrong Seniority — if it's too junior (analyst, coordinator, specialist)
  • Wrong Company — if the company is irrelevant
  • Other — type your reason and press Enter (e.g., "pharma - wrong industry")
  • Ideal — perfect match for Peter! Type WHY it's ideal and press Enter. Use for top companies (Revolut, Kraken, Binance, McKinsey) + right seniority (Director, VP, Head of)

Your feedback directly improves the AI scoring model — the more you mark, the smarter it gets!

Tabs explained:

  • Easy Apply (Peter) — these are for Peter only, don't touch
  • Cherry tab — your performance stats and daily target
  • Applied — jobs you've already marked as done

Email Trick for Applications:

  • Use the Gmail +trick when applying: peter+companyname@gmail.com (e.g., peter+revolut@gmail.com, peter+binance@gmail.com)
  • This is especially important when applying to multiple positions at the same company — use different +tags like peter+revolut1@gmail.com, peter+revolut2@gmail.com
  • All emails still arrive in Peter's inbox, but it helps track which application is which
  • For Indeed jobs (marked INMCP or INJS): you can log in to Indeed using Peter's .work email to apply directly
